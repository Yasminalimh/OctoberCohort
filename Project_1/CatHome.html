We are a small, volunteer run, no-kill cat charity

We rescue cats and kittens , and with your help we treat them and find them loving new

forerver home

Cat home is a non-profit, no-kill charity in Stockholm established in 2018 to rescue cats. We are run entirely by a small team

of dedicated (unpaid) volunteers whose aim is to help cats in need, especially those that need it receive medical treatment,

need special care or are elderly

We operate using a network of foster carers and our trustees cover all other running costs. This allows us to use 100% of

donations for medical expenses, specialty medical food, medicine and on a few occasions is used for food and litter.
